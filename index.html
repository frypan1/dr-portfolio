Professional Summary

ENT Surgeon (H.O.D) - King Fahad Specialist Hospital

Special Interests: Tympanoplasties, Tracheostomies and Endoscopy procedures, Myringotomies, Septoplasties, Adenotonsillectomies, Nasal polypectomies


    Since 1987 (Feb) worked as Senior Registrar in KFSH Buraidah till 1998. Since March 98, I am working as an ENT consultant and head of the Department of ENT in Buraidah Central Hospital. BCH is A secondary care hospital, and is the oldest general hospital in Gassim region. It has capacity of 400 bed in which all surgical and medical specialities are represented. The ENT department has three ENT consultants and 5 specialist and holds daily clinic session for two times. Average 30-35 patients are seen in the clinic everyday. Three separate operation days with an average of 15-20 cases per week is done excluding the emergencies. The bulk of operative work consists of adeno-tonsillectomies, nasal polypectomies, septoplasties, myringotomies, tympanoplasties, tracheostomiesand endoscopy proceedures, done by each doctor individually on his days. In hospital referrals from attached hospitals like Maternity and Child Hoapital, Chest Hospital and Psychiatry Hospital are also seen by us.
